History

40 Years Farm Traditions

Since

1985

In the quaint countryside of Brussels, a visionary named Henri Redwood founded Red Rooster Farm in 1985. With a passion for sustainable agriculture and a dream of providing the freshest produce to the community, Henri transformed a modest plot into a thriving agricultural haven. Over the decades, the farm expanded its offerings to include a diverse range of fruits, vegetables, dairy, and poultry, becoming a beloved local institution.

Since

1987

In the 1985s, the farm faced a whimsical challenge when a group of friendly roosters made it their home. Rather than shooing them away, Henri embraced their presence, dubbing them the "Red Roosters" and adopting one as the farm's mascot. This charming twist added a unique touch to the farm's identity.
